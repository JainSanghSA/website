About Us

Jain Sangh South Africa is a spiritual and cultural community dedicated to the teachings of Lord Mahavir. We aim to foster unity, compassion, and ethical living through regular Navkar Mantra Jaap, educational programs (Digital Jain Pathshala), and community service.
