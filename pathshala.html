About Pathshala

Our Digital Jain Pathshala offers online classes and resources to help children and adults learn about Jain philosophy, values, and practices from anywhere in South Africa.

Features

  • Weekly online classes
  • Interactive learning materials
  • Qualified teachers
  • Open to all age groups
